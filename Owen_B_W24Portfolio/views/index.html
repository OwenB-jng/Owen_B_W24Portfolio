About Me

Hello! My name is Owen Beattie, and I am a software developer studying at George Brown College. I've grown up surrounded by computers all my life, and have always been interested in how they work. I love Math and Science, and while I lack many the usual artistic talent that it takes to write or draw, I've always been relatively creative. In coding, I have found a way to mix these two passions, which was not easy since the logic of math and creativity of art seem to be at odds with each other. I love being able to see a problem and find unique ways to solve it, which will often differ greatly from the approach of my peers! That's another reason I love programming, the collaboration that it requires. Not many people would be able to solve complex problems on their own, and coming together as a group really inspires me to learn more every day! I love programming, and I'm exicted to share where this journey takes me.
